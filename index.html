Getting Started with Your Trezor Device

Welcome to Trezor.io/Start速. Here you will find all the information you need to set up and use your Trezor device. Follow these steps to ensure your device is secure and ready to use:

  1. Connect your Trezor device to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  2. Open the Trezor Suite application on your computer.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your device, including creating a new wallet and setting up a PIN.
  4. Ensure you write down your recovery seed and store it in a safe place.
  5. Once setup is complete, you can start managing your cryptocurrencies securely.
